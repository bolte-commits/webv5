Posting Guidelines

  • Posting time: 8–9 AM or 6–7 PM (test both)
  • Every post should end with a CTA pointing to the link in bio (booking page)
  • Stories: Post on non-story days too — reshare the feed post with a “New post” sticker
  • Hashtags: #DEXAscan #BodyComposition #BodyInsight #KnowYourBody #FitnessData #BeyondTheScale #BodyFat #LeanMass #VisceralFat #HealthData

Client Photo Tips

  • Always get written permission before posting
  • Tag clients — it boosts reach and makes them feel valued
  • Keep captions about their experience, not their body
  • Avoid before/after body shots unless the client specifically wants it — focus on the data story
  • Ask every client at checkout: “Mind if we grab a quick photo?”

Data Drop Guidelines

  • Always anonymize — never tie a stat to an identifiable person
  • Round numbers for readability (“over 60%” not “62.7%”)
  • Use phrases like “from our database of 1,000+ scans” — it builds authority
  • If you’re the largest database in your market, say it: “The largest DEXA dataset in [city/region]”

Partnership Content Tips

  • Always get approval from the partner organization before posting — clear any logos, names, and photos
  • Lead with credibility, not bragging — let the partnership speak for itself
  • Tie every partnership post back to the reader: “The same science the pros use — available to you”
  • For IPL/BCCI content, avoid sharing specific player data — keep it general and aspirational
  • Gym partnerships should highlight member benefits, not just the gym’s name
  • Use partnership posts to also seed B2B interest — “Want to bring DEXA to your facility? DM us.”
  • Rotate between different partners — don’t over-index on any single one

Brand Voice

  • Clean, confident, data-driven — not bro-science
  • Approachable but premium
  • Let the report speak for itself — it’s your biggest differentiator

Design Guidelines (Carousel / Static Posts)

  • Dimensions: 1080 × 1440px (3:4 portrait)
  • Background: Dark (#0F0F0F) with blue gradient accents — not flat dark, add depth with gradients and glows
  • Fonts: Syne 800 for “BODY INSIGHT” logo ONLY. Monument Extended (700/800) for all headings and display text. DM Sans for body text and buttons.
  • Colors: White text on dark. Accent blue #0A84FF on dark backgrounds (text, borders, solid fills, rgba glows). #007aff only on light backgrounds (e.g. text on white buttons) and in CTA slide gradients. Used boldly — blue background blocks, thick blue left borders on cards, solid blue icon circles, blue gradient fills on accent words, blue-tinted card backgrounds (rgba(0,122,255,0.06–0.15))
  • Logo: “BODY INSIGHT” in Syne 800 at 28px, centered top of every slide, low opacity (rgba 0.3)
  • Cards: Blue-tinted backgrounds with 4px blue left border, 20–24px border radius. No meaningless icons — let bold labels and descriptions do the work.
  • Step numbers: White text inside solid blue circles. Step titles in blue, descriptions in light text.
  • Hero slides: Use radial blue glow behind text for depth. Use solid color #0A84FF for accent words (not gradient text — html2canvas can’t render background-clip: text).
  • CTA slide: Blue gradient background (#0062cc → #007aff → #3898ff) with white radial glow. White pill button for URL.
  • Body text opacity: rgba(255,255,255,0.85) for body copy and descriptions. rgba(255,255,255,0.7) for subtexts and secondary labels. Never go below 0.7 for anything meant to be read.
  • Font sizes (at 1080×1440): Hero text 86px, section titles 56px, card labels 32px, body/descriptions 26–28px, subtext/pills 28–36px, CTA headline 72px, CTA button 36px
  • Spacing: 80px slide padding. No gaps between slides when viewing as a strip.
  • Scan duration: 6 minutes (not 7)
  • Background rule: Always dark for designed/graphic posts (carousels, data drops, statics). Client spotlight photos may use a lighter or neutral treatment so the photo stands out, but all branded graphics stay dark + blue.
