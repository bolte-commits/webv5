DEXA is the only scan that measures bone mineral density with clinical precision — catching bone loss years before a fracture.

80%

Bone density peaks around age 30 and declines after. Early detection changes outcomes dramatically.

Hormonal Health

PCOS, Menopause & Body Composition

Hormonal changes at every life stage affect where and how women store fat. DEXA reveals visceral fat — the metabolically dangerous fat that often increases during perimenopause, even without weight gain on the scale.

Visceral Fat

The Hidden Risk

Women with normal BMI can still carry high visceral fat — linked to heart disease, Type 2 diabetes, and metabolic syndrome. DEXA quantifies it precisely. BMI cannot.

Muscle Health

Sarcopenia Starts Earlier Than You Think

Women lose muscle mass 2x faster after 50. Tracking lean mass through DEXA helps design the right exercise and nutrition plan before it’s too late.
