The Problem

Your scale is lying to you

Weight and BMI hide what actually matters. Smart watches and BIA scales fluctuate 5-15% based on hydration alone. You can't manage what you can't measure accurately.

The Truth

What you're missing today

A person weighing 70 kg could be metabolically healthy or pre-diabetic — weight can't tell you. BMI labels muscular athletes "overweight" and misses dangerous visceral fat in "normal weight" individuals.

What DEXA reveals

DEXA uses two low-dose X-ray beams to map fat, lean tissue, and bone with ±1% accuracy — region by region. Used by BCCI, Olympic centres, and research institutions worldwide.
