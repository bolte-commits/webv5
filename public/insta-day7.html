What These Numbers Mean
4 metrics. One complete picture.
Visceral Fat Mass
Total weight of fat surrounding your organs. Linked to heart disease and diabetes.
Visceral Fat Volume
How much space that organ fat occupies. More volume, more pressure on your organs.
Visceral Fat Area
Cross-sectional spread. The clinical risk marker used by researchers worldwide.
Subcutaneous Fat
Fat under your skin. Less dangerous, but the ratio to visceral fat matters.
